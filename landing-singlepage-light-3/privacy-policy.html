Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, and company name when you create an account.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, and activity logs to improve your experience.
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, and device identifiers for security and analytics.
  • Communication Data: Any messages, feedback, or support inquiries you send us.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain our platform.
  • Personalize your experience.
  • Improve features and functionality.
  • Send updates, service announcements, or promotional content (you can opt out).
  • Respond to support requests and inquiries.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We only share your information with:

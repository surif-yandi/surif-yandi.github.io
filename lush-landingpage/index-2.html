About Us

Lush Development Group

Lush Development Group (LDG) is a property developer and real estate agency based in Bali, Indonesia. LDG offers residences that embrace the concept of “mindful luxury”. Mindful luxury is the result of a process that integrates design and atmosphere. This process uses all available resources to achieve the desired psychological elements. It's not just about living the high life. For us, luxury is all about sustainability, responsibility, and living mindfully.
